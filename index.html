PROJECTS

Post Societal Environment

Meticulously crafted world of our post-societal environment, a sprawling cityscape brought to life through meticulous modeling, texturing, and Unreal Engine wizardry. Drawing inspiration from the iconic skyline of New York City and the majestic allure of Berlin's dome. Marvel at the towering skyscrapers that stretch towards the sky, while the centerpiece—a modern interpretation of Berlin's dome—stands as a beacon of unity and aspiration amidst the urban landscape.

Kashmir Village

Crafted village nestled in Unreal Engine's digital. Inspired by the enchanting landscapes of Kashmir, every aspect of this virtual haven has been meticulously modeled, textured. From the rustic charm of traditional architecture to the lush foliage adorning winding pathways, every detail invites exploration and discovery, this meticulously crafted environment promises an unforgettable journey through the ethereal allure of Kashmiri-inspired splendor.

MEC

3D game asset that draws inspiration from the iconic aesthetics of the Matrix movie universe. This designed mech exudes a perfect blend of sleek sophistication and raw power, boasting angular contours and glowing accents that evoke a sense of cutting-edge technology. From its imposing stature to the intricate details of its cybernetic components, every aspect of this creation is crafted to immerse players in a world where man and machine converge in a thrilling battle for supremacy. Whether standing sentinel amidst urban landscapes or engaged in high-octane combat, this mech asset promises to elevate any gaming experience with its unmistakable Matrix-inspired flair.

Make Shift Drone

Make-Shift Dorne emerges as a testament to resilience, where salvaged materials from ruined cities are repurposed to construct makeshift shelters and fortifications against the unforgiving elements and roaming threats. Tattered banners flutter in the wind, signaling the unity of disparate survivors who have banded together in this desert wasteland, their homes rising like defiant monuments amidst the vast expanse of sand and ruin. Each structure tells a story of adaptation and resourcefulness, blending ancient architectural motifs with modern ingenuity, embodying the indomitable spirit of those who refuse to surrender to the ravages of a fallen world.

Sci-fi Environment

Step into a dystopian future where the remnants of human civilization cling desperately to survival amidst the cold, metallic corridors of a derelict space station. Inspired by the haunting atmosphere of "Alien Isolation," this meticulously crafted sci-fi environment immerses you in a world of eerie solitude and imminent danger.With Unreal Engine's powerful capabilities bringing this vision to life, prepare to embark on a journey that blurs the lines between reality and nightmare, where every corner may conceal the lurking terror of the unknown.
